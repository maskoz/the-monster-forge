What it does

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

⚔

Six Combat Roles

Brute, Soldier, Skirmisher, Artillery, Controller, Support — each with its own stat priorities, weapons, and villain actions.

◆

Villain Action Cards

Three-round narrative arc: Setup → Twist → Ultimate. Drawn from phase-tagged tables so every round has the right escalation.

☠

Bloodied Escalations

Every ability has a half-HP escalation. Fights change shape as the monster takes damage — no two combats feel the same.

◇

On-Hit Riders

Universal effects drawn from Damage Rider, Movement Trick, and Battlefield Control tables — weighted for variety.

✦

Creature Flavor

120 role-specific physical descriptions. Each NPC arrives with a one-line portrait the GM can read aloud immediately.

⬡

Batch Generation

Forge 1–10 NPCs at once. Names auto-generate per role, or supply your own. Named batches get numbered suffixes automatically.

In Practice

What a forged NPC looks like

Every NPC is written directly into the Foundry description tab — ready to run without prep.

Seven feet of knotted sinew, neck wider than its head, moving with the slow patience of something that has never lost a fight.

The Monster Forge v10.14 — Level 5 (Tier 2) | Role: Brute

AC 13 | HP 26 | ATK 2 atk +4 (1d8) | DC 15

Weapons: Greataxe (melee), Javelin (ranged)

Mods — STR 2 DEX 1 CON 1 INT −1 WIS 0 CHA 0

◆ Round 1 — Setup:

Grab and Hurl. The brute seizes the nearest creature and hurls it up to Near. The thrown creature lands Prone.

☠ Bloodied: The thrown creature crashes into another creature within Close of the landing point, and both fall Prone.

◆ Round 2 — Twist:

Ground Slam. The brute smashes the ground — a Close radius of difficult terrain erupts, all creatures within Close knocked back 5 feet.

☠ Bloodied: The radius extends to Near and all creatures within it fall Prone.

◆ Round 3 — Ultimate:

Whirlwind Strike. The brute spins in a full circle — all creatures within Close take 1d8 damage and are pushed back 5 feet.

☠ Bloodied: The reach extends to Near.

◇ On-Hit Rider:

Bleeding Cut. On a hit the wound refuses to close — the target loses 1 HP at the start of each of its turns until bandaged.

☠ Bloodied: Bleeding damage increases to 1d8.

Get Started

Install in Foundry VTT

Open Add-on Modules → Install Module and paste the manifest URL below.

Manifest https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maskoz/the-monster-forge/main/module.json

Or download the zip directly from the Releases page and extract into your Data/modules/ folder.
